Library Rules & Etiquette
  • Take only what you need to ensure supplies remain for all neighbors[cite: 1].
  • Respect the space—keep it tidy and organized[cite: 1].
  • Close the doors firmly after use to protect seeds and books from moisture and pests[cite: 1].
  • Donations are encouraged; please ensure they are in good, usable condition[cite: 1].

Native Plants, TEK, and Research

Our focus is on BC native plants, invasive species management (Japanese Beetle, spongy moth), and integrating Traditional Ecological Knowledge (TEK) through "two-eyed seeing"[cite: 1, 2].

Learn more about our ongoing research project, "Roots of Resilience," exploring the industrial and social history of the Burrardview area[cite: 1, 2].
