Welcome, Neighbor!

This is a micro-library and seed exchange serving the heart of Surrey. We are a space for learners, gardeners, and dreamers. Proudly funded by a 2026 Neighbourhood Small Grant.

Library Etiquette

To keep this resource thriving, please follow these guidelines:

  • Take what you need, leave what you can: Sustainability is our goal.
  • Close the door: Our seeds and books are sensitive to the elements.
  • Respect the space: Treat the library as an extension of your own garden[cite: 1].
  • Donation Quality: Only donate books in readable condition and seeds that are clearly labeled[cite: 1].
